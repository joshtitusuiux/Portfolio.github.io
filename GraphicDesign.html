Graphically Redesigning Church Newsletter

Overview:
I doubled the click-rate from 1.4% to 2.8% from the
previous month's newsletter by re-designing the Cross
Roads Church's parent newsletter. Cross Roads Church
has a newsletter they send out to 100+ church parents
and asked me to design a new newsletter to get
those who receive it excited to attend church events.












Creation and Design Choices:
I applied the design principles of hierarchy and contrast to draw the eye from top left to bottom right of the newsletter. I used Adobe Photoshop to place text onto a non-copyright image, and cut a semi-circle out of the bottom of the image before making it transparent so the bottom of the image became a curved overlay over the white newsletter.










Timeline:
November 11, 2023 - November 14, 2023.
My Role:
Graphic Designer, UI Designer, Visual Designer
Tools:
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Indesign, MailChimp



Reception:
The newsletter received multiple positive testimonials
praising the interesting design.
